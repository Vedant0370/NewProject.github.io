WHO WE ARE

One of the eminent names in the Cattle Feed Manufacturing Industry, the company has state of the art facilities at Ahmednagar with a manufacturing capacity of 540 metric tonnes of livestock feed per day. We diligently make it a point to source high quality ingredients in order to be able to produce premium and superbly nutritious livestock feed so that your animals give the best performance. We strongly believe that - ‘true success lies in the progress of our customers, suppliers, business partners and the community.’ Our day – to – day operations are based on the same ethos. Supported by our core values of integrity and transparency, we provide our customers with a wide range of animal nutrition solutions to help them gain profits in their businesses.
